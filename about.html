About

My Story

My name is Paige Mariucci and I am a designer based in San Francisco. I have a curiosity for discovering how things work, drive to solve problems, and appreciation for effective designs.

I spent the past decade working in architecture which provided me with an understanding of usable design as well as a respect for the design process. As a result of wanting to reach more people with my work along with a desire for a faster paced & more iterative product, I was inspired to apply my existing skills towards a career in user experience design.

In my work I prefer to approach projects by getting an understanding of the big picture in order to strategically determine what steps and tasks need to be accomplished. I am especially motivated by working in teams of passionate people to crack challenging problems. Plus, I know there's always more to learn.

Thanks for getting to know me! Please feel free to reach out if you want to work on a problem together.
